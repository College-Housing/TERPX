Why TERPX?

Moving can be stressful, expensive, and time consuming! TERPX will give you a peace of mind when it comes to buying, selling, or exchanging furniture, school materials and supplies, and even exchanging your lease with another student through a simple, easy, and trusted process available only to University of Maryland students so that you can have a smoother, cost-effective, and time efficient moving experience.

TERPX BENEFITS

  • Join a trusted community of UMD Students
  • Save money on buying used items
  • Save time finding what you are looking for
  • Exchange your lease with another student at no cost
  • Get all the above benefits completely free of charge!

FAQ

Join a trusted community of UMD students and begin exchanging right away in three easy steps:

1.Do I have to be a UMD student to Join TERPX?
  • Yes
2.Which UMD campus is part of TERPX?
  • TERPX is available for all UMD campuses
3.Is there a cost for using TERPX?
  • No. TERPX is available completely free of charge for UMD students.
4.What can I sell or exchange?
  • Furniture.
  • School materials and supplies
  • Lease agreements (Another student can replace you on the lease)
5.How can I pay for my purchases?
  • You will contact the student directly and discuss the payment method that works for you. TERPX does not interfere in how payments between students are made.
6.How the lease exchange works?
  • Once you find another student who agrees to replace you on the lease if you need to leave, simply send College Housing an email request at contact@collegehousing.us with the name and email of the student replacing you. A representative will then contact the student and guides them through the process.
